About Sakura Sushi

Crafting authentic Japanese sushi with passion, precision, and tradition since 2012.

Our Story

Sakura Sushi was founded with a simple mission: bring authentic Japanese sushi to our local community using fresh ingredients and traditional techniques. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved restaurant known for quality, creativity, and unforgettable flavor.
